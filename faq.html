  Q: How do I apply for a job at Sweet Treats?
  -To apply for a job,just mail at helpwanted@Sweettreats.com with your   resume,mentioning the position for which you want to apply.

  Q: Can Sweet Treats donate to my charity?
  -We strive to produce the highest quality baked goods at the highest   level of responsibility and partner with charities whose commitment to   their passions matches ours.

  Q: Can I customize my cake order?
  - Yes, cakes and cupcakes can be decorated with edible images,   inscriptions and you can mention your requirements while ordering.   Some cakes can't be decorated because of thier textures of icing like   Coconut,German Chocolate, Icebox cake, Flourless Chocolate cake and   Lemon Vanilla Bundt cake.

  Q: What are your shipping rates and time?
  -At Sweet Treats,everything is made fresh from scratch using the finest   ingredients. Since we bake to order, we cannot ship same day.So,the   cakes can be delivered in 24 hrs.but other items like flower   bouquets,snacks,sweets will be delivered in 3 hrs from the time of   ordering.

   Q: What if I am not satisfied with my order and want to cancel it?
  -If there is a problem with an order you received, please call 9878986785   or email us at sweetonline@treatsbakery.com.you can cancel your order   by login and clicking on cancel order option.Then you can order for   another product.
