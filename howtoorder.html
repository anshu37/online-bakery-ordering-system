How To place an Order

Our shop "Sweet Treats" allows users to check and purchase various products available online. It consists of various bakery products displayed in various categories and the user can browse through the items. The customers can order instantaneously across anywhere around the area, anywhere they want. The Online Bakery Ordering System will be computerized so that it has less paper work to do which makes it easy for both buyer and seller. Customers don't have to face the hassle of packing and the crowd formed in front of the items displayed and long queue at the billing counter. It will save both time and effort.

The customers need to go to the view products section in the home page. They will get different menus of cakes,snacks,sweets,combos,flower bouquets. Customers can order the product by just clicking on "Order Now" button for their desired product.It will show the description and price of the product. Then,they will get an option to login to their account.After customer log in to his/her account,they can make the payment via credit card. Cash on delivery feature is also available.
