  Sweet Treats opened its first location on a quiet street corner in the heart of Kolkata City's West   Village. From its inception, Sweet Treats has been cherished for its classic American baked   goods,vintage decor and warm, inviting atmosphere.


  Sweet Treats original owner passed her oven mitts to Steve and Tyra Abrams. Together, the   Abrams have thoughtfully expanded the bakery from its first West Village shop to locations in the   town.Today, Sweet Treats can be found in Kasba New Market,Kolkata,West Bengal.   


  In addition to our current locations, Sweet Treats is trying to franchise internationally and   will soon open locations in Manila and Sao Paulo.Patrons are also invited to place pickup   orders for select products through our online store. We are also pleased to offer shipping for   a variety of products, including freshly baked cupcakes, cookies, brownies,flower   Bouquets,Combos,Special sweets of Different cities as well as corporate gift packages and   seasonal assortments.


  Our products also include Staple Breads such as Multigrain, Wholewheat, Sourdough as   well as wide range of Rustic Crusty Breads original to European daily cusine, which are   finding more and more appreciation from our customers. We offer a choice of Cakes,   Pastries,Confectionaries, Viennoiserie, Snacks and open range of Customised Gift, Festive   Hampers and... much more for Individual and Corporate needs.


  Currently we sell our products in Kasba New Market in South Conclave,   Rajdanga,Kolkata,West Bengal as well as in an online shop.
  With a dedicated and loyal clientele, we are ready to expand in the manufacturing and retail   business.
